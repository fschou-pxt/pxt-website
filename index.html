Our Story

Where domain expertise meets AI

PXT Tech Studio was born from a simple shift: AI and large language models have matured into agents capable of turning ideas into real products — no longer gated behind years of coding experience. That unlocks a new kind of cross-disciplinary collaboration, where specialists can bring technology directly into their own fields rather than waiting for someone to translate for them.

PXT was founded by a U.S. board-certified pediatrician, neonatologist, and developmental biologist whose career centers on developmental milestones, brain development, and learning. Using AI agents to bring that expertise together with technology is exactly the kind of collaboration PXT exists to make possible.

Our Work

In[Neo]Sight

Neonatology Insight

The scientific literature in newborn medicine is overwhelming — for new trainees, seasoned clinicians, and especially for families of critically ill infants. In[Neo]Sight is a tiered platform that explains cutting-edge studies in language each audience can readily absorb, improving both care and understanding while making it easier for new ideas to be tested.

Every article is produced through a multi-agent approach designed to ensure accuracy and integrity, and reviewed by board-certified physicians. In[Neo]Sight also builds practical tools for the field, and will grow into a publication platform welcoming neonatal-perinatal research in data science, AI/LLM, and technology to improve clinical care.
